Who I am

Developer with a passion for frontend

Hello and welcome to my portfolio! My name is Angela Efremova, and I am a recent graduate of George Brown College's Computer Programming and Anlysis program. With a passion for front-end development, I've spent countless hours honing my skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I'm always looking for new and innovative ways to create beautiful and functional user experiences.

But my interests don't stop there - when I'm not coding, I love to travel and explore new places. I find that traveling helps me stay open-minded and creative, which in turn helps me come up with fresh ideas and approaches to my work.

In this portfolio, you'll find examples of my front-end development projects, ranging from simple websites to more complex applications. I'm excited to share my work with you, and I hope you enjoy exploring it as much as I enjoyed creating it. Thanks for stopping by!
