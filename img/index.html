Who I am

Developer with a passion for UX/UI Design

Hello and welcome to my portfolio! I'm Angela Efremova, a recent graduate from George Brown College's Computer Programming and Analysis program. My passion lies in UX/UI design, where I thrive on crafting seamless digital experiences.

My focus centers on wireframing, prototyping, and creating visually appealing interfaces. I enjoy working with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, constantly seeking innovative ways to blend aesthetics and functionality.

Beyond design, I'm a curious traveler, always seeking fresh inspiration from new places. These experiences enrich my creative thinking and influence my design choices.

In this portfolio, you'll find my collection of UX/UI design projects. From intuitive interfaces to intricate applications, I'm excited to share these pieces with you. I hope you find as much enjoyment exploring them as I did bringing them to life. Thank you for dropping by!
